About Us

NBPDCL is a company registered under the provisions of Companies Act 1956 and is a fully owned subsidiary Company of BSPHCL. The Company is engaged primarily in the business of distribution of Electricity. North Bihar Power Distribution Company Limited is a public sector undertaking (PSU) controlled by the Government of Bihar. It was formed on 1 November 2012 under section 14 of the Electricity Act of 2003, and is the successor to the erstwhile Bihar State Electricity Board.
